## Version Numbering Guide

### Format: MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH

- **MAJOR** (2.x.x): Significant redesigns, brand repositioning, or breaking changes
- **MINOR** (x.1.x): New features, pages, or substantial content updates
- **PATCH** (x.x.1): Bug fixes, small content updates, performance improvements

### Examples
- `2.0.0` - Complete revamp (current)
- `2.1.0` - Add new service page or major feature
- `2.1.1` - Fix typo or broken link

---

## Release Schedule

- **Major Releases**: Annually or as needed for rebrand
- **Minor Releases**: Quarterly (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
- **Patch Releases**: As needed (bug fixes, urgent updates)
